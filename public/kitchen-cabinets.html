    Custom solutions starting from GHC 10,000 - prices vary based on materials and project scope

    Solid Hardwood

    Mahogany, Oak, and Walnut options that develop beautiful patina over time. Perfect for traditional Ghanaian kitchen designs.

    • 10-15 year lifespan
    • Natural grain patterns
    • Can be refinished multiple times
    • Excellent durability
    • Classic aesthetic appeal
    Projects from GHC 15,000

    High-Grade Laminate

    Moisture-resistant and budget-friendly with extensive color options. Our most popular choice for Kumasi apartments.

    • 5-8 year lifespan
    • 100+ color options
    • Easy maintenance
    • Resistant to humidity
    • Budget-friendly
    Projects from GHC 10,000

    Premium Plywood

    Moisture-resistant cores with veneer finishes. The ideal balance between quality and value for Ashanti Region homes.

    • 8-12 year lifespan
    • Warp-resistant
    • Lightweight yet strong
    • Variety of finishes
    • Good moisture resistance
    Projects from GHC 12,000
