Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical kitchen cabinet installation take?

The duration depends on the complexity of the project, but most standard kitchen cabinet installations take between 2-5 days. Custom designs or larger projects may take 1-2 weeks from start to completion.

What materials do you use for your cabinets?

We use high-quality materials including solid wood (mahogany, oak, and walnut), plywood, and MDF with various finishes. We can advise you on the best material for your specific needs and budget.

Do you offer design consultations?

Yes, we provide free initial design consultations where we discuss your needs, take measurements, and provide recommendations. For more complex projects, we can create detailed 3D renderings of the proposed design.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Kumasi but serve clients throughout the Ashanti Region and across Ghana. For projects outside Kumasi, transportation costs may apply depending on the location.

How do I maintain my cabinets?

We recommend regular dusting with a soft cloth and cleaning with a mild detergent solution. Avoid abrasive cleaners. For wood cabinets, occasional polishing with appropriate wood polish will maintain the finish. We provide specific care instructions for each material we use.
