    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about our custom wardrobes

    How long does a custom wardrobe installation take?

    The timeline depends on the complexity of your project, but most standard wardrobe installations take between 3-7 days. Walk-in wardrobes or more complex designs may take 1-2 weeks from measurement to completion.

    What materials do you use for wardrobes?

    We use high-quality materials including solid wood (mahogany, oak, and walnut), plywood, and MDF with various finishes. We'll advise you on the best material for your specific needs, budget, and style preferences.

    Can you work with existing wardrobes?

    Yes, we can refurbish or modify existing wardrobes with new doors, interiors, or finishes. During our consultation, we'll assess your current setup and recommend the best approach.

    What's included in the free consultation?

    Our free consultation includes an on-site visit to take measurements, discuss your needs and preferences, provide design recommendations, and give you a preliminary estimate. For complex projects, we may create 3D renderings of the proposed design.

    How do I maintain my custom wardrobe?

    We recommend regular dusting with a soft cloth and cleaning with a mild detergent solution. Avoid abrasive cleaners. For wood wardrobes, occasional polishing with appropriate wood polish will maintain the finish. We provide specific care instructions for each material we use.
