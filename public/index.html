About Edward Sarpong Enterprise

Edward Sarpong Enterprise has been serving Kumasi and the Ashanti Region for decades, providing premium custom cabinetry and interior solutions. Our passion for craftsmanship and attention to detail sets us apart in the industry.

We combine traditional woodworking techniques with modern design to create functional, beautiful spaces that reflect our clients' personalities and lifestyles.

Quality Craftsmanship

Every piece is built to last with premium materials and expert construction techniques.

Custom Designs

Tailored solutions that perfectly fit your space, needs, and personal style.

Nationwide Service

Serving clients throughout Ghana with professional installation and support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

The duration depends on the complexity of the project, but most standard kitchen cabinet installations take between 2-5 days. Custom designs or larger projects may take 1-2 weeks from start to completion. We provide a detailed timeline during our free consultation.

We use high-quality materials including solid wood (mahogany, oak, and walnut), plywood, and MDF with various finishes. We can advise you on the best material for your specific needs, budget, and lifestyle during our consultation.

Yes, we provide free initial design consultations where we discuss your needs, take measurements, and provide recommendations. For more complex projects, we can create detailed 3D renderings of the proposed design to help you visualize the final result.

We're based in Kumasi but serve clients throughout the Ashanti Region and across Ghana. For projects outside Kumasi, transportation costs may apply depending on the location. Contact us to discuss your specific location requirements.

We recommend regular dusting with a soft cloth and cleaning with a mild detergent solution. Avoid abrasive cleaners.
